Classic Fine Foods Values Quiz


Welcome to Our Values Quiz Project!

We may come from all around the world, but at Classic Fine Foods we speak one language - our core values.

This fun quiz explores these values in the languages spoken by the members of our values project group.

Why not test your knowledge and see if you know the core values in French, Portuguese, Hungarian, Burmese, Italian and Maori!

Even though we may all have our own mother tongues, working at Classic Fine Foods brings us all together and our core values make us One CFF.

As you progress through the quiz, piece by piece, you will uncover the mosaic of our shared values.

Enjoy the challenge and discovery!
